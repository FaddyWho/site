Who am I

Fahad Ali.

Location

Karachi, Pakistan

Occupation

Visual Designer

Background

I was born in Islamabad but raised in Larkano, Sindh, and spent much of my early life moving from one place to another. This constant change exposed me to diverse cultures and experiences, fueling my passion for music, art, and gaming. Over time, these interests converged into a love for creative expression and problem-solving. I pursued architecture at the National College of Arts, where I graduated with a degree that deepened my appreciation for design and its impact. Beyond my academic pursuits, I enjoy playing table tennis, badminton and e-sports . Today, I work as a multidisciplinary visual artist, blending my diverse skills and experiences to craft engaging and innovative visual narratives.
