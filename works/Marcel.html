Introduction

Client shared multiple visual references for the look and feel of the brand they required. The theme was mainly based on World of Warcraft game, and mainly something known as Eye of N'Zoth.

Graphics

Logo

I based the logo on the eye of N'Zoth to create two logo iterations. While the idea was to mix flat modern styles with retro semi 3D visual style similar to WOW's style. The client and I decided to go for a flat modern design as it would be much more legible on smaller screen and easy to brand on different assets.
